Why This Blog Exists

February 15, 2026

This is the first post on GregoryGingeleskie.com.

I am building this site as a permanent home for essays I write, with the help of AI, on topics I find interesting: technology, civilization, power, economics, and the long arc of human development. These essays are a small effort to contribute to the body of global knowledge from which future intelligence will be derived.

The site is intentionally simple - thoughts and writing.
